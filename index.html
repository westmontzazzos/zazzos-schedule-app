SCHEDULING RULES
Official rules enforced by the schedule checker
🍕 Pizza Cook Rules
All days: Must have opener (by 9AM) · Must have closer (C)
Mon–Thu: Minimum 2 cooks · At least 2 working between 4–8PM
Fri–Sat: Minimum 6 cooks
AM shifts must have an end time
PM shifts with no end time default to 8PM
🔪 Kitchen Cook Rules
All days: Must have opener (by 9AM) · Must have closer (C)
Sun–Thu: Minimum 2 cooks · At least 2 working between 4–8PM
Fri–Sat: Minimum 3 cooks · At least 2 working between 4–8PM
AM shifts must have an end time
PM shifts with no end time default to 8PM
🧹 Prep Rules
Must have opener (by 9AM)
AM shifts must have an end time
🍽️ Bus / Dish Rules
Dish must have a closer (C)
AM shifts must have an end time
🍸 Bartender Rules
Must have opener at or before 10:30AM
Must have closer (C)
AM shifts must have an end time
💵 Cashier Rules
Must have opener before 11AM
Mon–Thu: Minimum 1 cashier scheduled
Fri–Sat: Minimum 3 cashiers working between 4–8PM
AM shifts must have an end time
🪑 Host Rules
Must have 1 host on: Mon, Tue, Wed, Fri, Sat, Sun
(Thursday is off — no host required)
If an AM host shift exists, it must have an end time
🍽️ Server Rules
Must have closer (C) any day servers are scheduled
Fri & Sat: Minimum 5 servers starting 3:30PM or later
Sunday: Minimum 4 servers starting 3:30PM or later
AM shifts must have an end time
🚗 Driver Rules
Exactly 2 AM drivers starting before 11AM — every day
Exactly 1 closer (C) — every day
⚠️ Override Policy
If a rule cannot be followed, tap ⚠️ OVERRIDE RULE on the error.
You will be required to enter your PIN and acknowledge the rule being broken.
Overrides suppress the error for that week only.
